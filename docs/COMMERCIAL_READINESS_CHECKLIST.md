## Recommended Sales Path

1. Sell as an internal research automation pilot, not as a guaranteed alpha product.
2. Offer a 2-4 week pilot with 25-50 tickers.
3. Measure analyst time saved and report quality.
4. Run historical validation on the firm's preferred coverage universe.
5. Convert to paid deployment after data-source and model-governance review.
